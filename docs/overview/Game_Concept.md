# 🎨 Game Concept

## Elevator Pitch
A turn-based art market simulation where players build collections, speculate on emerging artists, navigate social circles, and grow their wealth — all within a compressed, fast-moving version of the real art world.

---

## Core Loop

```
Choose Character → Start with Assets/Capital
        ↓
Advance Turn (1 week in-game)
        ↓
Market Fluctuates → Events Trigger → Dialogue Choices
        ↓
Buy / Sell / Hold / Attend Events
        ↓
Portfolio Grows (or shrinks) → Repeat
```

---

## Design Pillars

1. **Compressed Time** — Years pass quickly. Players feel the weight of long-term strategy without the real-world wait.
2. **Social Capital Matters** — Who you know affects what you can access and at what price.
3. **Risk vs. Patience** — Holding is powerful but costs opportunity. Timing is everything.
4. **Character Identity** — Your starting persona shapes your entire experience and available strategies.
5. **Emergent Narrative** — Random life events (inheritance, scandal, death, bonus) make each run feel unique.
