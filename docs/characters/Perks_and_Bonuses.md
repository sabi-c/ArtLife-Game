# ⭐ Perks & Bonuses

## Overview
Beyond starting class perks, players can **unlock additional perks** as they progress — through relationships, achievements, and in-game events.

---

## Unlockable Perks

### Social Perks
| Perk | How to Unlock | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Inner Circle** | Attend 10 gallery events | Access to invitation-only sales |
| **Trusted Collector** | Buy from same gallery 5x | First refusal on new works |
| **Art Fair Regular** | Attend 3 major fairs | Reduced commission on fair purchases |
| **Philanthropist** | Donate a work to institution | +reputation, unlock institutional events |

### Financial Perks
| Perk | How to Unlock | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Freeport Account** | Store 3+ works in freeport | Reduced storage fees |
| **Art-Backed Loan** | Hold 500k+ portfolio | Borrow against collection value |
| **Tax Efficient** | Use freeport 5x | Further reduced tax on sales |

### Market Perks
| Perk | How to Unlock | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Eye for Talent** | Discover 3 artists before Heat > 40 | See emerging artist signals earlier |
| **Auction House Favourite** | Win 5 auctions | Reduced buyer's premium |
| **Contrarian** | Buy during a bear market | Bonus value multiplier on those works |

---

## Annual Events (Class-Specific Bonuses)

| Class | Annual Bonus | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Hedge Fund Manager | Cash bonus (variable, 10–30% of starting capital) | Every 52 turns |
| Rich Kid | Random estate event | Every 60–80 turns |
| Gallery Insider | Exclusive artist access | Every Art Basel cycle (~52 turns) |
| Speculator | "Hot tip" intel card | Every 20–30 turns |
