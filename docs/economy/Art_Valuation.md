# 💰 Art Valuation

## Overview
Every work in the game has a dynamic value driven by a combination of artist heat, market conditions, provenance, and rarity. Understanding valuation is the core financial skill of the game.

---

## Valuation Formula (Simplified)

```
Work Value = Base Price × Artist Heat Multiplier × Market Multiplier × Rarity Factor × Provenance Bonus
```

---

## Components Explained

### Base Price
- Set at time of creation / first sale
- Determined by artist's tier at time of work's entry into the market
- Never changes — it's the anchor

### Artist Heat Multiplier
| Heat Score | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| 0–20 | 0.5x – 0.8x |
| 21–40 | 0.9x – 1.2x |
| 41–60 | 1.3x – 1.8x |
| 61–80 | 2x – 4x |
| 81–100 | 5x – 10x+ |

### Market Multiplier
| Market State | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Bull | 1.2x – 1.5x |
| Flat | 1.0x |
| Bear | 0.6x – 0.8x |

### Rarity Factor
- **Edition works** (prints, photos): lower rarity, more liquid
- **Unique works** (paintings, sculpture): higher rarity, less liquid but higher upside
- **Early works** (pre-heat): retrospectively valuable if artist breaks out

### Provenance Bonus
- Previously owned by a famous collector: +10–25% premium
- Exhibited at major institution: +5–15% premium
- Featured in major publication: +5–10% premium
- Provenance builds over time and stacks

---

## Buy vs. Sell Spread

| Channel | Buy Price | Sell Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary gallery | Market rate | N/A (gallery sells) | Access gated by relationship |
| Secondary market / private | Negotiated | Negotiated | Best for discreet transactions |
| Auction (major) | Hammer + 25% buyer's premium | Hammer – 15% seller's commission | Public, sets market comps |
| Auction (distressed) | Below market | — | Opportunity buys |
| Freeport-to-freeport | Negotiated | Negotiated | No tax, fully private |

---

## Price Discovery
- Auction results are **public** — every sale sets a comparable for that artist
- Private sales are **invisible** — but rumours can surface via events
- Over-paying at auction inflates your own holdings' value (can be strategic)
- Under-paying signals weakness in an artist's market

---

## Liquidity by Work Type

| Type | Liquidity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Blue-chip, major artist | High | Always a buyer, tight spread |
| Hot emerging artist | Medium-High | Fast moving, timing sensitive |
| Mid-career, established | Medium | Slow but predictable |
| Unknown / speculative | Low | Hard to sell without taking a loss |
| Distressed / inherited | Variable | Depends on circumstance |
