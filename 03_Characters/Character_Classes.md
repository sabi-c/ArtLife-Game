# 🧑 Character Classes

## Overview
At the start of the game, the player selects a character archetype. Each class provides a **unique starting position**, **capital level**, **special perks**, and **ongoing mechanics** that shape the entire playthrough.

---

## The Classes

### 💰 The Rich Kid
> *"Your father built the collection. Now it's yours."*

- **Starting Capital:** Medium cash + inherited collection (5–10 works)
- **Starting Perk:** Begins with established works — no need to build from scratch
- **Ongoing Mechanic:** Random life events (inheritance windfalls, family drama, trust fund payouts)
- **Key Event:** Father dies of heart attack mid-game — triggers large one-time collection or cash injection
- **Weakness:** No insider knowledge, collection may be old-school taste

---

### 📊 The Hedge Fund Manager
> *"Art is just another asset class."*

- **Starting Capital:** High cash, no collection
- **Starting Perk:** Strong analytical view of market data
- **Ongoing Mechanic:** **Annual bonus** every ~52 turns — significant cash injection to deploy
- **Key Advantage:** Can absorb losses more easily, plays long positions
- **Weakness:** Socially excluded from some gallery tiers — seen as "just a buyer"

---

### 🖼️ The Gallery Insider
> *"You've been in the room before anyone else."*

- **Starting Capital:** Low cash, strong relationships
- **Starting Perk:** Early access to artists before public market, private sales at cost
- **Ongoing Mechanic:** Receives **advance notice** of gallery openings and artist breakouts
- **Key Advantage:** Best access to emerging artists before heat builds
- **Weakness:** Lower capital, must be careful with social relationships (can't offend galleries)

---

### 🎲 The Speculator
> *"Buy cheap, sell fast, repeat."*

- **Starting Capital:** Medium cash
- **Starting Perk:** Access to distressed sales and undervalued works
- **Ongoing Mechanic:** Higher swing on wins AND losses — volatility is the mechanic
- **Key Advantage:** Can turn quick profits that others can't
- **Weakness:** Reputation risk if seen as a "flipper" — some galleries refuse to sell

---

### 🏛️ The Curator (Stretch Goal)
> *"You understand what matters."*

- **Starting Capital:** Very low, but high cultural credibility
- **Starting Perk:** Can influence artist heat scores through institutional attention
- **Ongoing Mechanic:** Curating a show for an artist boosts their Heat — and your holdings
- **Weakness:** Ethical conflict mechanic — using position for personal gain has consequences
