# ⚙️ Turn Engine

## What Happens Each Turn

### Phase Order Per Turn

```
1. PLAYER ACTION PHASE
   └── Buy, Sell, Hold, Attend Events, Make Decisions

2. ADVANCE WEEK (player clicks)
   └── Game locks — no actions

3. RESOLUTION PHASE (background)
   ├── Market prices update
   ├── Artist heat scores shift
   ├── NPC relationships age
   ├── Scheduled events trigger
   └── Income/bonuses processed

4. EVENT DELIVERY PHASE
   ├── 0–3 events surface as notifications/cards
   └── Dialogue choices presented if required

5. NEW WEEK BEGINS
   └── Player can act again
```

---

## Event Frequency
- **Common** (every 1–3 turns): small market movements, minor news
- **Uncommon** (every 4–8 turns): gallery openings, cocktail parties
- **Rare** (every 20–52 turns): major life events, windfalls, market crashes, death of key figures
- **Annual** (every ~52 turns): Art Basel, major auctions, bonuses (for relevant characters)

---

## Player Action Options Per Turn
- 📦 **Buy** — purchase a work from gallery, auction, or private sale
- 💸 **Sell** — list or accept an offer on a work
- 🔒 **Hold** — do nothing with a piece (passively appreciates or depreciates)
- 🗓️ **Attend Event** — go to a gallery opening, party, or fair (costs time slot)
- 🏦 **Move to Freeport** — store work tax-efficiently (costs fees, reduces liquidity)
- 🤝 **Network** — spend social capital to build NPC relationships
