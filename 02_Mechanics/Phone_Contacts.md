# 📱 Phone & Contacts System

> The Nokia flip phone is the player's primary communication hub. Messages arrive from NPCs, market alerts come through, and relationships are managed here.

---

## Phone Mechanic

The phone sits in the right panel of the game hub. It has three functions:

### Messages
- NPCs send messages between turns (1-2 per week)
- Messages have **urgency levels**: urgent (respond now), normal (respond soon), low (whenever)
- Each message has **actionable responses** that affect stats and NPC favor
- Ignoring messages has consequences (missed deals, offended NPCs)
- Message categories: deal, invitation, intel, personal, alert

### Contacts
- 11 NPCs across 6 roles: dealers, gallerists, auction house, artists, collectors, advisors
- Each has a **favor score** (-100 to +100) that shifts based on player actions
- High-favor NPCs offer better deals, earlier access, exclusive info
- Low-favor NPCs freeze you out, bad-mouth you, drive up prices

### Calendar
- Shows upcoming art world events (fairs, auctions, biennales, social seasons)
- Events are based on the real annual art calendar
- 16 seasonal events mapped to specific months
