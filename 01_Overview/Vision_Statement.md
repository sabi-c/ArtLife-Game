# 🔭 Vision Statement

## What is this game about?
The art market is one of the most opaque, relationship-driven, and speculative markets in the world. This game makes that world playable — exposing the absurdity, strategy, and excitement of collecting, flipping, and hoarding art across a compressed timeline.

---

## Tone
- Smart but accessible
- Darkly comedic (e.g. "your father dies of a heart attack — but he left you his Basquiats")
- Aspirational and satirical at the same time

---

## Target Audience
- Art world insiders who want to see their world gamified
- Finance/strategy game players who want a fresh setting
- Casual players attracted to the aesthetic and social simulation elements

---

## What makes this different?
- Set in the **contemporary art world** — a rarely explored game setting
- **Character class system** tied to real-world art world archetypes
- Mechanics built around **real art market behaviours** (freeports, gallery tiers, auction houses, blue-chip vs emerging)
- Turn-based but with **meaningful time compression** — decisions feel consequential

---
